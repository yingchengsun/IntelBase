Multi site Growth Over Time

Growth over time highlights the trend from the beginning of deployment until today as well as the trend over the last 90 days.

The number shown is the average loss or gain per day over the period.

For Example:

  • A Positive Number indicates that the store has experienced an average daily increase for the period.
  • A Negative Number indicates that the store has experienced an average daily decrease for the period.


A positive overall number with a negative 90 day number is a casue for concernin that there has been growth over time but a loss over the past 90 days. Lack of intervention may lead to continued loss.
